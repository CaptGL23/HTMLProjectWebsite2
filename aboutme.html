10/03/20, Daniel Litten, Unit 2: A Little About Myself

Growing up

I am originally from Northern Ohio, about 30 minutes from Cleveland, Ohio. I started playing baseball when I was 3 years old and continued playing until I was 17. A lot of my free time growing up consisted of skateboarding, drawing, playing video games, and playing basketball for fun.

Adult Life

I met my wife when we were in high school and now we have been together almost 10 years and have 3 beautiful children. After high school, I attended The University of Toledo to get a Bachelor's degree in astrophysics. At the same time, I was attending the Reserve Officers' Training Corps (ROTC) at Bowling Green State University. With having a wife and child, I couldn't afford to stay in school so my wife and I decided it was best if I enlisted in the Air Force and worked towards a degree. Six years later and here we are, finally working towards that degree.

My Hobbies

Gaming is really my only hobby, outside of work and family. One of the games I normally play is League of Legends. I tend to stream sometimes, if I have the chance. If you want to check out my stream or videos, the links will be below. When I can't play, I normally watch professional play.
